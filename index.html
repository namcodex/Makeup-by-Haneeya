FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your appointment.

Fill out the booking form above with your event details. I will contact you within 24 hours to confirm your appointment. A reservation fee is then required to officially secure your date.

The reservation fee is ₱500 to ₱1,500 depending on the package. This is non-refundable but is deducted from your total balance. It ensures your date is locked in and no other client can take it.

Yes. I offer home service with a travel fee starting at ₱500 for Metro Manila areas. For provincial bookings, travel and accommodation fees apply and are discussed upon confirmation. I cover most major cities nationwide.

Rescheduling is allowed up to 7 days before your event, subject to availability. Cancellations made within 48 hours of the event forfeit the reservation fee. Please communicate with me as early as possible.

Standard makeup takes 1.5 to 2 hours. Bridal full glam takes 2.5 to 4 hours including hairstyling. Please plan accordingly and communicate your call time so I can schedule the proper window.

I use only professional-grade products from brands such as MAC, NARS, Charlotte Tilbury, and Make Up For Ever. Please disclose any skin allergies or sensitivities in your booking form so I can adjust my product selection accordingly.

Yes. Entourage makeup is available as an add-on service starting at ₱2,000 per additional person. For large parties, please inquire early as I may need to coordinate additional artists to cover the team.
