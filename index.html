About Haneeya

Soft Glam, Made for Mindanao

Hi, I'm Haneeya — known online as HMUA · Makeup by Haneeya. I specialize in bridal glam, engagement and prenup looks, and elegant hijab draping for brides who want a soft, natural, and long-lasting finish.

Based in Marawi City, I take bookings across Lanao del Sur, Lanao del Norte, Iligan City, and Cagayan de Oro. My approach blends gentle contouring and clean base work with eye definition that photographs beautifully — perfect for your wedding day, engagement shoot, or any special celebration.

Slots are limited every month, so brides usually message ahead to secure their date.
